FAQ

Common Questions

Are you available for advisory or board positions?

I selectively take on advisory roles for startups building in African markets, particularly those focused on commerce, fintech, or marketplace platforms. If you think there's a good fit, please reach out with details about your company and what you're looking for in an advisor.

Do you speak at events and conferences?

Yes, I enjoy speaking about entrepreneurship in Africa, building technology for local markets, and the future of digital commerce. I've spoken at various tech conferences and business events. Please send details about your event including dates, audience, and topic expectations.

How can I join or partner with Market360?

We're always looking for talented people and strategic partners. For job opportunities, please check our Market360 careers page. For partnership inquiries, use the contact form above and select "Partnership Opportunity" as the subject. Vice CEO Ezekiel Sesay and I review all partnership proposals personally.

What is your typical response time?

I typically respond to messages within 2-3 business days. For urgent matters related to Market360 or time-sensitive opportunities, please indicate so in your message and I'll prioritize accordingly.
