Our Story

What is DevLink?

DevLink is a premier software agency headquartered in Sierra Leone, dedicated to delivering world-class digital solutions for businesses of all sizes. We specialize in web development, mobile applications, UI/UX design, custom software, and API integrations that power modern businesses.

Founded with a mission to bridge the technology gap in West Africa, DevLink brings together a talented network of developers, designers, and strategists who are passionate about building software that makes a real difference. We work closely with startups, SMEs, and enterprises to turn ideas into scalable, high-quality digital products.

Our approach combines modern development practices with deep local market understanding. We don't just write code — we craft experiences that resonate with users and drive measurable business outcomes. From MVPs to enterprise platforms, DevLink is the trusted partner for organizations across Sierra Leone and beyond.

As a developer-focused agency, we are also committed to growing the tech ecosystem in Africa. We mentor emerging developers, contribute to open-source projects, and actively foster a community where talent can thrive and innovate.

Our Journey

The DevLink Timeline

Key milestones in DevLink's journey from a bold idea to Sierra Leone's trusted software agency.

2025 - Present

Scaling Across West Africa

Expanding service coverage to 6 major cities across Sierra Leone. Growing the team to 15+ members and taking on enterprise-level projects for organizations across the region.

2024

Launching Mobile & MVP Services

Introduced mobile application development and rapid MVP building services. Delivered 20+ projects and established partnerships with startups and SMEs across Freetown and beyond.

2023

First Major Client Projects

Completed our first wave of commercial projects — including e-commerce platforms, business websites, and API integrations. Built a reputation for quality and reliability.

2022

Building the Team

Assembled a core team of developers, designers, and project managers. Established our development workflow, quality standards, and client onboarding process.

2021

DevLink Founded

DevLink was born from a vision to deliver world-class software solutions from Sierra Leone. Started with freelance web development projects and a commitment to excellence.
