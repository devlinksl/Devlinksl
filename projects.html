Digital Marketplace for Sierra Leone

Market360 is Sierra Leone's leading digital marketplace, connecting local businesses with customers across the nation. Built from the ground up to address the unique challenges of African e-commerce.

Solution: A comprehensive e-commerce platform enabling merchants to list products and customers to shop locally with ease.
